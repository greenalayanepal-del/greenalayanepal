About This Resource

This comprehensive photographic collection documents the rich butterfly diversity of the Kathmandu Valley, one of Nepal's most biodiverse urban regions. The document features high-resolution images of over 127 butterfly species, including detailed information about their habitats, seasonal patterns, and conservation status.

This resource serves as a valuable tool for researchers, students, conservationists, and nature enthusiasts working to understand and protect Nepal's Lepidoptera diversity. Each species is documented with multiple photographs showing different angles, life stages, and natural habitats across various locations in the valley.
