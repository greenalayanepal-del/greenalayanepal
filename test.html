Who We Are

National Environmental NGO for Research & Innovation

Greenalaya Nepal is a national environmental NGO that leverages research, technological innovation, and green enterprise to deliver data-driven solutions for resilient ecosystems through collaboration.

We work at the intersection of nature and technology, empowering communities to conserve and restore ecosystems while fostering sustainable green innovative enterprises in balance with nature.

Our Vision

A resilient Nepal where empowered communities conserve ecosystems and foster sustainable green innovative enterprises in balance with nature and technology.

Our Mission

To generate credible environmental knowledge, advance community-centered conservation, and catalyze innovative eco-business solutions that protect biodiversity, strengthen local livelihoods, and influence sustainable development pathways in Nepal.

Community-Led Conservation

Empowering local communities through participatory conservation and capacity building

Our Foundation

Core Strategic Pillars

Five interconnected pillars guide all our initiatives, ensuring holistic and sustainable environmental solutions for Nepal.

People

Empowering communities through education, training, and participatory conservation

Technology

Leveraging AI, GIS, and data systems for smart environmental monitoring

Research

Conducting credible environmental research to inform evidence-based action

Nature

Protecting and restoring ecosystems, biodiversity, and natural resources

Collaboration

Partnering with communities, organizations, and government for systemic change

What We Do

Thematic Areas of Focus

Seven key thematic areas drive our work, addressing emerging environmental challenges through innovation, research, and community engagement.

01

Emerging Environmental Issues & Research

Addressing new and understudied challenges

  • Microplastics detection & research
  • Urban biodiversity mapping
  • Invasive species management
  • AI for sustainable urban biodiversity
  • Pollution hotspot mapping
02

Conservation & Ecosystem Restoration

Protecting and rehabilitating habitats

  • Forest micro-pond programs
  • Water recharge structures in Chure hills
  • Degraded ecosystem mapping
  • Biodiversity corridors
  • Pollinator parks & bee conservation
03

Climate Change & Pollution Management

Adaptation, mitigation, and reduction

  • Community carbon literacy programs
  • Early warning systems
  • River cleanup programs
  • Carbon footprint training
  • Pollution status analysis
04

Innovation, Eco-Products & Circular Economy

Linking conservation with livelihoods

  • Local product branding (wild honey, sarus grains)
  • Eco-packaging solutions
  • Biodegradable alternatives
  • Biomimicry product design
  • Green enterprise prototypes
05

Environmental Technology & Data Systems

GIS, AI, and data-driven solutions

  • Digital mapping through GIS
  • Remote sensing & R programming
  • Smart pollution tracking tools
  • Environmental monitoring systems
  • Digital biodiversity documentation
06

Community Conservation & Capacity Building

Empowering local communities

  • Citizen scientist programs
  • Green classroom in schools
  • Local ranger training
  • Eco-tourism hub promotion
  • Skill sharing workshops
07

Environmental Policy, Governance & Ethics

Science-based policies and advocacy

  • Policy research & gap analysis
  • Evidence-based recommendations
  • Government engagement
  • Ethical governance standards
  • Advocacy campaigns
