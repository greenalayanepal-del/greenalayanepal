About Us

Greenalaya Nepal is a non-profit organization dedicated to protecting nature, empowering communities, and promoting sustainable practices across Nepal. We work with local communities, youth, and partners to create long-term environmental impact.

Our Programs

Environmental Conservation

Tree plantation, biodiversity protection, and ecosystem restoration initiatives across rural and urban Nepal.

Climate Awareness

Education programs, workshops, and campaigns to raise awareness about climate change and sustainable living.

Community Development

Supporting local communities with eco-friendly livelihoods, waste management, and green innovation.
